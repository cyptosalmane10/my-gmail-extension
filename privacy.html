Privacy Policy for "Containers Manager"

Last Updated: July 12, 2025

1. Data We Collect

To provide its services, the Containers Manager extension requests read-only access to your Gmail account using the gmail.readonly scope.

  • We do not store your email content or any part of it on our servers. Ever.
  • The only data saved are the authentication tokens (Access & Refresh Tokens) provided by Google. This data is stored securely and locally on your browser using the browser.storage.local API.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the read-only permission solely to display your email information within the extension's interface. The only purpose is to provide you with convenient access to your inbox. Your data is not analyzed or used for any other purpose.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any of your data or access tokens with any third party.
